Our Story

We believe you deserve to know what's in your food.

DecodeFood was born from a simple frustration — food labels are intentionally hard to read. We're changing that.

Our Mission

We combine barcode scanning, AI analysis, and open food databases to give every person — regardless of their chemistry background — a clear, honest breakdown of what they're eating.

No jargon. No fearmongering. Just facts, context, and actionable insights.

How It Works

📷

Scan or Upload

Point your camera at any barcode or upload a photo of the ingredients label. NOTE: not all food products are in the database.

🔍

Database Lookup

We query Open Food Facts — a crowd-sourced database of 3M+ products — for ingredients data.

🧠

AI Analysis

Gemini AI reads the ingredient list and produces a plain-English health verdict with a score, concerns(including allergens), and potential benefits.

✅

You Decide

Armed with clear information, you make an informed choice about what goes in your body.
