1. Not Medical Advice

DecodeFood is designed for educational and informational purposes only. The health summaries, scores, and insights provided by this application do not constitute professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always seek the advice of your physician, nutritionist, or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition, dietary restrictions, or nutrition planning.

2. AI Limitations & Accuracy

The analysis provided by DecodeFood is generated dynamically using Large Language Models (LLMs) like Gemini and Nemotron. While these models are highly capable, AI can make mistakes, omit context, or hallucinate information.

Do not blindly trust the AI verdict. It is an interpretation of text, not a laboratory analysis of the actual physical food product.

⚠️

3. Allergens & Crowdsourced Data

Our barcode scanner relies on OpenFoodFacts.org, a free and open crowdsourced database. Product formulations change frequently, and user-submitted data may be incomplete, outdated, or entirely incorrect.

If you have severe or life-threatening allergies (e.g., peanuts, gluten, dairy), you must ALWAYS read the physical packaging of the product before consumption. DecodeFood cannot guarantee the absence of allergens.
